Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025-01-01

1. What we collect

With your consent, we may process: EEG heuristics, keyboard cadence, webcam fatigue signals, and app usage. Raw media is not uploaded; derived metrics are used for on-device guidance whenever possible.

2. How we use data

  • To compute focus windows, stress/drift gauges, and gentle recommendations.
  • To improve the product in aggregate (opt-in only).

5. Third-party services

We use essential infrastructure (e.g., CDN) that does not profile users. No ads. No selling of personal data. Ever.
